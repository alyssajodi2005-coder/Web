About Our Mission

Our Neighborhood Mission is a community-centered nonprofit focused on supporting children, families, and individuals of all abilities through advocacy, outreach, and inclusive community care.

Founded by Chanel Blake, the organization was created to connect families and individuals to the resources, support, and opportunities they deserve.
