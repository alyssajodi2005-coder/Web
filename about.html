About Us

Our mission, story, and purpose

Our Mission

Our Neighborhood Mission is dedicated to building a supportive community for children and adults while advocating for individuals with disabilities. We aim to provide resources, opportunities, and programs that empower families, strengthen neighborhoods, and create inclusive spaces where everyone can thrive.

"Our Neighborhood Mission was created to be a bridge, connecting individuals and families to the resources, support, and opportunities they deserve."

Chanel Blake

About Me

Hi, I’m Chanel Blake, founder of Our Neighborhood Mission.

I was raised in the very community I now serve, and giving back has always been close to my heart. With a background in community services and healthcare, I created this organization to connect families to support, opportunity, and the resources they deserve.

This work is personal to me as a mother, neighbor, and advocate who believes strong communities create lasting change for generations to come.

"Many communities do not lack strength, they lack access."

Chanel Blake
