Our Mission

Our Neighborhood Mission is dedicated to building a supportive community for children and adults while advocating for individuals with disabilities. We aim to provide resources, opportunities, and programs that empower families, strengthen neighborhoods, and create inclusive spaces where everyone can thrive.

About Me

Hi, I’m Chanel Blake, founder of Our Neighborhood Mission.

I was raised in the very community I now serve, and giving back has always been close to my heart. Growing up, I saw firsthand how powerful support, opportunity, and access to resources can be in shaping lives. That experience stayed with me and became the foundation for the work I do today.

With a background in community services and healthcare, I’ve spent years helping families navigate challenges, from housing and stability to caregiving and rehabilitation. Through those experiences, I realized that many communities do not lack strength, they lack access. That is where Our Neighborhood Mission comes in.

Our Neighborhood Mission was created to be a bridge, connecting individuals and families to the resources, support, and opportunities they deserve. Whether it is through food drives, community events, youth programs, or life-skills development, our goal is simple: to uplift, empower, and build stronger neighborhoods together.

This is not just an organization. It is personal. I am a mother, a neighbor, and someone who truly believes that when we invest in our communities, we create lasting change for generations to come.
