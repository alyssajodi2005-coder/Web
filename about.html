About Our Mission

Who we are and what guides the organization.

Our Mission

Our Neighborhood Mission is dedicated to building a supportive community for children and adults while advocating for individuals with disabilities. We aim to provide resources, opportunities, and programs that empower families, strengthen neighborhoods, and create inclusive spaces where everyone can thrive.

Founder Spotlight

Chanel Blake is a Certified Occupational Therapy Assistant and community advocate based in Harlem. Her work is rooted in creating welcoming spaces for children and families and making sure support is accessible, practical, and community-centered.
